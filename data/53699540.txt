 Rixty Minutes is the eighth episode of the first season of Rick and Morty It premiered on Adult Swim on March The episode was written by Tom Kauffman and Justin Roiland and directed by Bryan Newton In the episode Rick and Morty watch cable from other dimensions while Jerry Beth and Summer watch alternate reality versions of themselves using a pair of interdimensional goggles The episode was well
